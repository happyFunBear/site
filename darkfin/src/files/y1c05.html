Year 1, Chapter 5: Off the Rails

All the principles rush to the train accident. As the authorities bicker and delay a response, Darkfin decides to ignite the oil slick before it can hit the bay. Meanwhile Morrigan spots a man in the wreckage and goes to help him. Darkfin charges her trident and throws it into the oil causing a huge fireball...

Off the Rails

Just past the intersection of Riverbank Drive and the 101, total chaos surrounds the scene as emergency personnel try to cordon off the area from onlookers. Six of the train's 17 cars are completely engulfed in flames as two others teeter precariously on the side of the bridge. A huge plume of oily dark smoke rises into the sky. The KCON news van arrives with the rest of the media.

Morrigan (Looking at the madness before her) Dear God!

Gracie (Speaking to the station) BENNY, ARE YOU GUYS READY OVER THERE? - WE'LL BE GOING LIVE IN TWO MINUTES... START THE COUNT

Morrigan heads for a police officer as Gracie sets up the shot.

MM OFFICER, OFFICER... could you please tell us what happened here?

Cop Sorry Ma'am, you're going to have to wait for our Public Information Officer just like everyone else

Morrigan Can you at least tell me if there have been any deaths?

Cop There have been fatalities - again, you'll have to wait for more details... now, I'm going to have to ask you two ladies to step back

Morrigan Thank you, officer

Zach comes rushing over.

Zach I just talked to one of my fireman buddies - they're going to start evacuating 10 square blocks of the surrounding area because of toxic smoke and fumes

Gracie Great, I just had the van washed!

Morrigan How bad is it?

Zach He said he particulate level is not that bad... yet - if this thing gets any bigger, people's lungs are going to be doing the "Benzene Boogie"

Gracie Morri, get ready

Morrigan (Into her mike) CHECK, CHECK... 1, 2... CHECK - they're not getting me, Gracie

Gracie (Banging on some equipment) There's too much interference for audio but the vid feed is fine - get on the cell to the talking heads and give them all of your info, they're just going to have to read over the shot!

Morrigan (Dialing the cell phone) If we move back about 100 yards, we might get some clearance

Gracie Yeah, but the studio wants me in as tight as possible on the shot

Morrigan No doubt - let me talk to the anchors and then I'll go find the DIS-information guy... let me know when we have audio

Gracie Will do!

Darkfin swims effortlessly up the river towards the spill, cutting through the water at upwards of 70 knots. Her sonic signature warns off the marine life in the area, clearing the river of fish. As she approaches the site, she can "taste" the change in the water. She stealthily rises from the water, concealed among some reeds and surveys the situation.

Darkfin I'm getting really tired of this

Piloting a USCG helicopter, Lt. Fiore flies over the scene with Captain Phelps next to her, barking orders into his radio.

Craig I WANT ALL WATERCRAFT OUT OF THE IMMEDIATE AREA, GET SOME TEAMS DOWN HERE TO START A CONTAINMENT BOOM AROUND THE OIL

Anita (Looking down below) NOTHING is going to contain that shit!

Craig (Turning off his radio) SPEAK A LITTLE LOUDER, FIORE, SO SOME ASSHOLE WITH A SCANNER CAN PICK UP THAT EXACT QUOTE

Anita Sorry - do you want me to go in closer?

Craig Nah, I can see all I need to see - remain at this level, we can coordinate better with this vantage point

Hidden below, Darkfin holds up her middle finger to the air.

Anita (Looking out at the rising smoke and growing oil slick) The wind speed is low, the spill is fresh and the water current is less than a knot... we have perfect conditions for an in-situ burn!

Craig There's a nearby residential area southeast of here

Anita The wind isn't blowing in that direction

Craig For now

Anita The conditions aren't going to get any better

Craig Fine, Anita, go ahead and click the red tape scissors shoes three times - let's see what happens

Anita Dammit, Craig, time is of the essence!

Craig There are too many variables and we need the Federal and State On-Scene Coordinators' permission with a residential area so close by

Anita IT'S THE FREAKING WEEKEND, THEY WON'T BE HERE FOR HOURS

Craig That's not our fault, is it?

Anita (Slamming her hand down on the panel) This is Alaskan North Slope Crude Oil, once it hits the bay, we're going to have another Exxon Valdez on our hands - it will take YEARS to clean up

CP At least someone will be getting an employment opportunity out of it

Anita HOW CAN YOU BE SO CAVALIER? - think of what it'll cost the people living here!

Craig THINK of our CAREERS going up in FLAMES if one person so much as COUGHS because of the fire WE set

Anita Save a few people... or an entire ecosystem - I say we choose the latter

Craig NOW who is the one being cavalier? - you've been hanging around your brother-in-law too much!

Anita At least Sandy, Shelby and Erika are trying to figure out ways to help

Craig Well, unless those science wizards are down there with magic wands, there's nothing any of us can do at this point

Darkfin (Clutching her trident and watching the slick spread towards the faster currents of the river) What are these fools waiting for, LIGHT IT

Calculating in her head and gauging the surrounding factors, Darkfin looks at the train.

Darkfin Just the right angle

On a cliff overhead, Morrigan and Zach question the Information Officer as the evening sky is obscured by flame and smoke.

Info Officer At this point, we can't rule out sabotage but we have no conclusive evidence yet and no further comment

Morrigan But one witness said there was a small explosion BEFORE the train derailed

Info Officer We have no confirmation of that yet - we're looking into faulty tracks, as well as, human error - it will take awhile before we know for sure

Zach At this time, there are two dead and three injured - all crew members of the train?

Info Officer That is correct

Morrigan Most of the cargo is from Meer-A-Mar Shipping, when can we expect to talk with them?

Info Officer They are helping set up a command center and will have a statement for you within the hour

Zach How is the evacuation going?

Info Officer Smoothly - a few people have been treated at local hospitals for smoke inhalation - but, as long as the wind continues to blow to the northwest, we don't foresee a problem

Out of the corner of her eye, Morrigan notices something in one of the wrecked cars by the bridge.

Morrigan Excuse me, I'll be right back

The young reporter snakes her way around the confusion and tries to get a closer look. Zach trails behind her.

Zach McBride, what are you doing?

Morrigan (Making her way down a side embankment) I think... IT IS... IT'S A MAN... HE'S TRAPPED BEHIND THE CAR

Zach I'LL GO FOR HELP

Morrigan (Now inching along the bridge towards the man) THERE'S NO TIME FOR THAT, THIS WHOLE BRIDGE IS ABOUT TO GO UP IN FLAMES

Zach DON'T DO IT, HE'S PROBABLY ALREADY DEAD

Morrigan WHAT IF HE ISN'T?

Zach (Now following cautiously behind her) FIRST RULE OF JOURNALISM, NEVER BE PART OF THE STORY

Morrigan FIRST RULE OF HUMANITY, ALWAYS HELP OTHERS

As Gracie pans the scene with her camera, she notices Morrigan and Zach.

Gracie (Zooming in) NO... NONONONO, WHAT IN THE HELL ARE THEY DOING?

Above, in the helicopter, Anita and Craig also spot the situation.

Craig (Screaming into his radio) WE'VE GOT CIVILIANS ON THE BRIDGE, GET THEM OUT OF THERE

Anita (Flying in closer) IT LOOKS LIKE ISAACSON AND MCBRIDE... CHRIST ALMIGHTY

With trident in hand, Darkfin takes a deep breath and positions herself carefully.

Darkfin It will still be a mess, but at least it's a mess that can be handled

The two reporters reach the man and start to carry him to safety. As they near the edge of the bridge, the man chokes and gasps.

Man Ahhgh... Pete... he's still inside... HELP HIM

Morrigan takes off back to the car, towards the smoke and flames.

Zach DON'T DO IT, THERE'S NOT ENOUGH TIME

Gracie (Watching through the camera lens) WHY IS SHE GOING BACK?

Anita (From the helicopter) JESUS, SHE'S GOING BACK

Darkfin bangs the bottom of her trident down hard on a rock to activate the charge. She prepares to throw it.

Darkfin Time's up

Inside the car, Morrigan looks frantically for another man.

Morrigan PETE... PETE... CAN YOU HEAR ME?

A small whimper is heard over the increasing roar of the flames. The woman finds a frightened Border Collie crawling towards her.

Morrigan Pete? - C'MON BOY, IT'S OK, C'MON

Grabbing the dog in her arms, Morrigan starts to run as fast as she can. Before she makes it off the bridge, everything in back of her explodes into flames.
